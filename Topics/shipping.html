Delivery Time
  1. We try to keep as many locks in stock as possible, however, when you get into custom items like Keyed Alike sets or Master Keyed systems, we will often have to have these manufactured when ordered. All in stock items will ship within 24 to 48 hours, while custom manufactured items will ship within 12 to 15 business days. If you are ordering locks that require manufacturing, you will be notified of the lead time.
  2. Our default shipping method is either FedEx Ground (commercial addresses) or FedEx Home Delivery (residential addresses). We also offer FedEx 2nd Day Air for when you need locks right away, however, keep in mind manufacturing times for custom items such as keyed alike locks. 

    Additionally, please note that delivery time is also based on "business" days.

Calculating Cost

Shipping costs are calculated on the website automatically during Checkout based on the weight of the items you are purchasing and your location.

Lock Express Program (Safety Lockout)

The Lock Express Program is available for products in the Safety Lockout category and offers custom built safety padlocks that will ship in 48 hours. If you are in need of Safety Lockout products in a hurry, please call us at 1-800-778-2217 and ask one of our customer service agents about the Lock Express Program.
